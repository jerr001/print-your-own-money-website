Gideon Ijidakinro

Helping thinkers and builders understand how value becomes income

What if value is created before money ever appears? Print Your Money is not about shortcuts, hype, or financial tricks. It is a reflective, practical invitation to rethink how money actually enters your life—through ideas, discipline, service, creativity, faith, and consistency.

Written in clear, human language, this book explores how mindset, learning, and purpose shape income long before a paycheck is printed. It challenges passive thinking and calls the reader to take responsibility for growth—personally, spiritually, and practically.
